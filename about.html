    MUKONO KINGS HIGH SCHOOL

Welcome to Our Education Website

Mukono Kings High School UNEB Center No. U2802 is a private mixed secular day and boarding secondary school. It has both "O" and "A" level. The school offers the Uganda National Curriculum at both "O" and "A" levels. It is fully registered with the Ministry of Education and Sports. We have a team of senior, fore-sighted, result oriented and highly proffessional teachers as center of Excellence to meet local and global Educational challenges.

Why Choose Our Institute

Mukono Kings High School propels its students to become top performers nationally and internationally, by adopting the best international and contemporary teaching and learning as well as educational leadership philosophies.

Academic Excellence

Mukono Kings High School is celebrated for its academic excellence, driven by a committed faculty and a dynamic curriculum.

Health & Psychology

Mukono Kings High School prioritizes student health, fostering a supportive environment that emphasizes physical well-being ands academic excellence.

Affordable Fees

Mukono Kings High School offers quality education with affordable school fees, making it accessible to a diverse range of students.

Science & Technology

Mukono Kings High School places a strong emphasis on Science and Technology education, equipping students with cutting-edge knowledge and practical skills.

Art & Music/Media

Mukono Kings High School values creativity and expression, offering vibrant programs in art, media, music, and drama. Students are encouraged to explore and develop their talents.

Cutting Edge Infrastructure

Mukono Kings High School stands out with cutting-edge infrastructure, featuring advanced science labs for hands-on learning, a comprehensive library for academic exploration, and top-notch sports facilities fostering physical well-being and teamwork.

Language

Mukono Kings High School recognizes the importance of language education, offering a diverse and comprehensive language program. Students are provided with opportunities to enhance their proficiency in multiple languages, fostering effective communication and global awareness. The school's language curriculum promotes linguistic skills that are essential for academic success and future career endeavors.

Web & Computer Services

Mukono Kings High School embraces the power of the internet as an integral tool for education. With a robust and high-speed internet infrastructure, students have access to a wealth of online resources, facilitating research, collaboration, and staying abreast of the latest developments. The school leverages technology to enhance the learning experience, preparing students for the digital age and fostering a culture of innovation.
